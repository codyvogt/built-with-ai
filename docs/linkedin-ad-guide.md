# LinkedIn Ad Setup Guide: Regina Case Study Campaign

## Campaign Overview

**Objective:** Drive applications to the free case study offer  
**Target:** Regina, SK business owners  
**Landing Page:** `https://builtwithai.ca/case-study.html`  
**Recommended Budget:** $25-30/day for 14 days ($350-420 total)

---

## Step 1: Go to LinkedIn Campaign Manager

1. Go to [linkedin.com/campaignmanager](https://www.linkedin.com/campaignmanager/)
2. Click **"Create Campaign"**

---

## Step 2: Choose Campaign Objective

- Select **"Website visits"** (sends traffic to your detailed application form)
- Alternative: "Lead generation" (collects info directly in LinkedIn, but less detail)

**Recommended: Website visits** → send to your case study page

---

## Step 3: Set Up Targeting

### Location
- Click "Locations" → Search **"Regina, Saskatchewan, Canada"**
- Expand radius to **"Within 40 km"** (catches White City, Pilot Butte, etc.)

### Company Size
- Click "Company" → "Company size"
- Select:
  - ✅ 2-10 employees
  - ✅ 11-50 employees
  - ✅ 51-200 employees

### Industries
Pick all that apply to your ideal clients:
- ✅ Construction
- ✅ Real Estate
- ✅ Agriculture
- ✅ Oil, Gas & Mining
- ✅ Professional Services
- ✅ Transportation & Logistics
- ✅ Manufacturing
- ✅ Healthcare Services

### Job Titles
Click "Job Experience" → "Job titles" and add:
- Owner
- Founder
- CEO
- President
- Managing Director
- General Manager
- Operations Manager
- COO
- IT Manager

**Alternative:** Use "Job Seniority" instead:
- Owner
- CXO
- VP
- Director

---

## Step 4: Ad Format

- Select **"Single Image Ad"** (simplest, works well)
- Or **"Video Ad"** if you create a short video later

---

## Step 5: Create Your Ad

### Ad Image Specs
- Size: **1200 x 627 pixels**
- Format: PNG or JPG
- File located at: `images/linkedin-ad-1200x627.png`

> Upload `images/linkedin-ad-1200x627.png` directly in the LinkedIn Campaign Manager image upload step.

### Headline (70 characters max)
```
FREE Custom Software for Regina Businesses
```

### Introductory Text
Copy and paste:
```
🎯 Regina Business Owners: We want to build your next software project — FREE.

Built with AI is looking for 2 local businesses to partner with on a case study.

Here's the deal:
✅ We build you custom software or an AI agent — completely free
✅ You own it 100%
✅ In exchange, we feature your project as a case study

Perfect if you need:
• A custom app or dashboard
• Business process automation
• An AI assistant for your team
• A tool to replace expensive subscriptions

Limited to 2 spots. Serious applicants only.

👇 Apply now
```

### Destination URL
```
https://builtwithai.ca/case-study.html
```

### Call-to-Action Button
- Select **"Apply"** or **"Learn More"**

---

## Step 6: Set Budget & Schedule

### Daily Budget
- Start with **$25-30/day**
- LinkedIn is more expensive than Facebook but higher quality B2B leads

### Schedule
- Run for **14 days** initially ($350-420 total test)
- Or set an end date and total budget

### Bidding
- Use **"Maximum delivery"** (automated) to start
- LinkedIn will optimize for you

---

## Step 7: Launch

1. Review everything
2. Click **"Launch Campaign"**
3. LinkedIn will review your ad (usually approved within 24 hours)

---

## Expected Results

| Metric | Expected Range |
|--------|----------------|
| Impressions | 5,000-15,000 |
| Clicks | 50-150 |
| Click-through rate | 0.5-1.5% |
| Applications | 3-10 quality leads |
| Cost per click | $2-6 |

---

## After Launch Checklist

- [ ] Check Campaign Manager daily for first 3 days
- [ ] Review which demographics are clicking (refine targeting)
- [ ] Respond to applications within 24 hours
- [ ] After 7 days, pause and assess results
- [ ] If working, continue. If not, adjust targeting or ad copy

## Troubleshooting

### Low impressions
- Expand location radius
- Add more industries
- Reduce job title specificity (use seniority instead)

### Low click-through rate
- Test different ad copy versions
- Make headline more specific
- Add urgency ("Only 2 spots", "Limited time")

### Clicks but no applications
- Check landing page loads properly
- Simplify form if possible
- Add more trust signals (testimonials, credentials)
