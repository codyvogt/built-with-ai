Questions?

Frequently Asked

Is this really free?

Yes. We're building our portfolio of local success stories. The only "payment" is permission to share your project as a case study on our website and social media.

What's the catch?

No catch. We're a new company building credibility. Case studies from real local businesses are worth more to us than the development cost right now.

Why only 2 spots?

We want to do this right. Each project gets our full attention. We're limiting to 2 so we can deliver exceptional results worth showcasing.

What if my project is too big?

We'll scope it during the discovery call. For this program, we're focused on small to medium projects (2-6 weeks of development). If yours is bigger, we can discuss a hybrid approach.

Do I need to be in Regina?

This particular offer is focused on Regina and surrounding area businesses. We want to build local relationships and local case studies first.

What happens after the project?

The software is yours. Period. If you want future updates or additional features down the road, we can discuss that separately — but there's no obligation.
